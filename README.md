# Gut Microbial Dysbiosis and Elevated Mucosal Inflammation in Severe Acute Malnutrition: A Case-Control Study from India

## Overview
This repository contains QIIME2 scripts used for processing 16S rRNA gene sequencing data generated in a matched case-control study of children with severe acute malnutrition (SAM) and healthy controls.

---

## Study Design
A total of 129 children aged 6–59 months were enrolled, including 57 children with severe acute malnutrition (SAM) and 72 healthy controls. Stool samples from 115 participants (52 SAM and 63 healthy controls) were subjected to microbiome analysis.

---

## Sequencing
- Platform: Illumina NovaSeq 6000  
- Chemistry: Paired-end 250 bp (PE250)  
- Target region: V3–V4 region of the 16S rRNA gene  
- Primers: 341F and 806R

---

## Data Availability
The sequencing data generated and analyzed during this study have been deposited in the NCBI Sequence Read Archive (SRA) under BioProject accession number PRJNA1402786.
